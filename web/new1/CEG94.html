IGNITE THE FUTURE

Graduates of 1994 batch from College of Engineering, Guindy have formed  the CEG94 Trust on 29 Nov 2017  to help the CEG community.

Over 1200 students graduate from 14 disciplines of engineering from this 225 year old meritorious institution which is home to eminent technocrats, entrepreneurs and statesmen .

Our Vision

Help CEG students from humble background to achieve their dreams

Our Mission

Financial aid to meritorious students in need

Provide career guidance through mentoring and skill development

Serve as a platform to connect our batchmates to students in need

Key Values

Want to Contribute ?

You can contribute in one of the following ways

Monetary
  1. Minimum contribution of ₹ 10,000/- requested from each 1994 alumni would go towards building a ₹30 Lacs corpus. Interest from this corpus would be used to sponsor students fees from AY2020-21
  2. Donors may also choose to specifically donate towards the near-term tuition and/or hostel fees for a particular academic year or semester. Any excess/unused amounts would be rolled forward to support subsequent semester(s) fees
  3. Donors giving repeat donations may need to inform the Trust (by email or via department champions) if they would like to support near-term fees, otherwise, by default, their contribution would be added to the corpus
Non-Monetary
  1. Conducting mock interviews
  2. Participating in mentoring and student development programs
